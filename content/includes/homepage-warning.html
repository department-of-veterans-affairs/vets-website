Some VA services in the Mid-Atlantic region and Pacific Islands may be affected by storms

If your usual VA facility is affected, you can contact another VA facility for help.

Veterans in the Mid-Atlantic region affected by Hurricane Florence can get updates on VA medical center closures and evacuations at the VA Office of Public and Intergovernmental Affairs website.

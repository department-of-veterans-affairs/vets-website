Some VA services in the Mid-Atlantic region and Pacific Islands may be affected by storms
If your usual VA facility is affected, you can contact another VA facility for help.

Veterans in the Mid-Atlantic region affected by Hurricane Florence can get updates on VA medical center closures and evacuations at our VAntage Point blog.
Get updates on affected VA services and facilities in the Mid-Atlantic region.

Veterans in the Pacific Islands affected by Typhoon Mangkhut and Tropical Storm Olivia can go to the VA Pacific Islands Health Care System website to get updates on VA medical center closures.
Get updates on affected VA services and facilities in the Pacific Islands.

Or you can call the Veterans Disaster Response Line at 1-800-507-4571.

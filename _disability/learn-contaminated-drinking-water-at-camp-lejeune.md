### Contaminated Drinking Water at Camp Lejeune

Between August 1953 and January 1988, two on-base water-supply systems at Marine Corps Base in Lejeune, North Carolina, were contaminated with the volatile organic compounds trichloroethylene (TCE), a metal degreaser, and perchloroethylene (PCE), a dry cleaning agent. Benzene, vinyl chloride, and other compounds were also found to be contaminating the water-supply systems. [Learn more about exposure to Camp Lejeune drinking water](http://benefits.va.gov/compensation/claims-postservice-exposures-camp_lejeune_water.asp)
